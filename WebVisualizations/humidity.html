    Humidity

    From the plot above, there's not a clear relationship that we can observe between the latitude and humidity: various humidity levels are present across different latitude levels. This is because there are some factors such as wind speed, air movement, cloud cover, precipitaion, air pressure, amount of vegetation, and amount of open work that are significant in determining the humidity of a place. However, we did observed a few 0% humidity spots between 60 to 80 degrees in the northern hemisphere. This could be resulted from a desert landscape where there's little precipitation.
